NVIDIA H100 - Deep Learning Powerhouse

The NVIDIA H100 with 80GB of GPU memory is the most powerful NVIDIA GPU and is 3-4 times faster than the A100. The Kinetic Cloud cluster equipped with 8 NVIDIA H100 GPUs are perfect for companies that are training or find tuning models with an excessive amount of data where optimal performance matters. NVIDIA’s H100 is fabricated on TSMC’s 4N process with 80 billion transistors and 395 billion parameters, offering up to 9x faster speed than the A100. “NVIDIA H100 is the first truly asynchronous GPU”, the team stated. The GPU extends A100’s ‘global-to-shared asynchronous transfers’ across the address spaces. It also grows the CUDA thread group hierarchy with a new level called the thread block cluster.

  • Train Foundation Models and LLMs with Lambda Cloud Clusters featuring NVIDIA H100
  • Fine Tune LLMs with large data sets.
  • The highest performance training available.

Kinietic Cloud Clusters that come with NVIDIA H100 Tensor Core GPUs deliver unprecedented performance, scalability, and security for every workload. NVIDIA H100 uses breakthrough innovations in the NVIDIA Hopper™ architecture to deliver industry-leading conversational AI and speeds up large language models.

NVIDIA A100 - Deep Learning Workhorse

Systems equipped with 8 NVIDIA A100 GPUs are immensely powerful and are ideal for a range of advanced AI-related tasks due to their high computational capabilities, extensive memory, and advanced features. A system with 8 NVIDIA A100 GPUs is a highly versatile and powerful configuration, well-suited to handling a variety of advanced AI tasks, from training sophisticated models to real-time inference and large-scale data analysis. The benefits stem from the advanced features, extensive memory, massive parallelism, and scalability of the A100 GPUs, enabling researchers and professionals to tackle complex problems and develop innovative solutions in various domains..

  • Fine Tune LLMs
  • Perform training for Generative AI
  • Combine with CPU Hosting for powerful inference.

The Kinetic Cloud A100 configuration utilizes NVLink and NVSwitch technologies for high-speed communication between GPUs, enabling efficient scaling for multi-GPU configurations. The platform design also enhances computation of AI workloads with dedicated Tensor Cores and support for mixed-precision computing, improving performance, and reducing training times. The system also supports features like hardware-based ray tracing, deep learning super sampling (DLSS), and advanced shading technologies. The NVIDIA A100 is built to handle demanding workloads and provide stable performance over extended periods of time..

NVIDIA A30 - Best Value for Inference

The NVIDIA A30 is designed as a versatile, high-performance GPU, catering to a variety of workloads, making it perfect for small training applications and running production inference. The main purpose of the 8xA30 system in the Kinetic Cloud is to provide a combination of CPU and GPU resources for production applications. The system as designed can support up to 24 virtual machines that include GPU resources plus an additional 120 CPU based virtual machines. The purpose of this configuration is to provide a “one-stop-shop” for businesses taking AI applications to production..

  • Perfect for Generative AI Inference
  • Available monthly in Hybrid VMs
  • Scale down to 1/3 GPU (8GB)

The A30 is optimized for a wide range of applications, from AI inference to graphics rendering, making it suitable for diverse customer needs in a GPU cloud hosting environment. With dedicated Tensor Cores, the A30 accelerates AI inference tasks, enabling fast, efficient processing of AI models, and thereby delivering high throughput for AI services. The A30 also supports multiple instances GPU (MIG). MIG allows the A30 to be partitioned into multiple instances, enabling isolation of workloads and optimal utilization of resources, which is critical to maximize resource allocation and serve multiple customers on a single GPU.
